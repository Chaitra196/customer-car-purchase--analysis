# 🚗 Austo Auto Mobile Company - Data Analysis Project

## 📌 Context
Analysts are required to explore data and derive meaningful insights. Clear communication is essential so that even non-technical stakeholders can understand the findings.

Auto Motor Company is a leading car manufacturer specializing in SUVs, Sedans, and Hatchbacks. Recently, concerns were raised about the efficiency of their marketing campaigns. The company decided to use data analysis to improve campaign performance.

---

## 🎯 Objective
The goal of this project is to analyze customer data to understand demand patterns and enhance customer experience.

As a Data Analyst, the task is to:
- Perform data analysis
- Answer key business questions
- Provide insights to improve decision-making

---

## 📊 Data Description
The dataset includes the following features:

- *Age*: Age of the individual (in years)  
- *Gender*: Male or Female  
- *Profession*: Occupation of the individual  
- *Marital_Status*: Married or Single  
- *Education*: Graduate or Post Graduate  
- *No_of_Dependents*: Number of dependents (children, parents, etc.)  
- *Personal_Loan*: Whether the individual has taken a personal loan (Yes/No)
- House_loan: A binary variable indicating whether the individual has taken a housing loan "Yes" or "No"
- Partner_working: A binary variable indicating whether the individual's partner is employed "Yes" or "No"
- Salary: The individual's salary or income.
- Partner_salary: The salary or income of the individual's partner, if applicable.
- Total_salary: The total combined salary of the individual and their partner (if applicable).
- Price: The price of a product or service.
- Make: The type of automobile
